About The

Allen & Sons Table Tennis Club

Our Facility

Built for practice, training, and open play

  • 8 ITTF-approved Butterfly Centerfold tables
  • Air-conditioned playing environment
  • Paddles and balls available
  • Advanced training robot system
  • Complimentary WiFi

The robot system allows players to train with consistent ball placement and automatic return, making it a great tool for improving technique, footwork, and repetition.
