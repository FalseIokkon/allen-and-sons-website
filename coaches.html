Bella Livshin

  • Coaching since 1968, making her our most experienced coach.
  • Won numerous US Open and US National titles in over-40, over-50, and over-60 groups.
  • Available to train players of all skill levels.

Vahid Mosaferi

Kim Gilbert

  • Kim is a professional table tennis champion and elite coach.
  • She is a three-time U.S. Olympic Festival Gold Medalist.
  • She currently holds the Olympic Record for the fastest reflexes

  • Kim is also the CEO of Pongstarz which produces and
    manages large scale ping pong themed events and celebrity fundraisers.

Rudy Miranda

  • Participated in 6 world championships, Germany (twice), China, Japan, Russia, and France.
